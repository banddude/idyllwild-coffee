## ðŸŽ¯ Key Opportunity

Idyllwild has 6 existing coffee shops, but all have significant weaknesses:
- **Alpaca Coffee:** Closes at noon
- **Black Mountain:** No espresso drinks, closed 3 days/week
- **Higher Grounds:** Customer service complaints
- **Pure Bean:** Inconsistent quality

**The gap:** No coffee shop open 6 AM - 7 PM, 7 days/week with quality products, excellent service, and comfortable gathering space.
